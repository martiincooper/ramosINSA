AIMS
The Multicriteria Decision Aid course covers methods and tools that help a decision-maker  make  a  complex  decision  involving  multiple  criteria,  often  contradictory  or  inconflict.
Three approaches will be covered in this course:- The AHP method (Analytic Hierarchy Process);- The TOPSIS method (Technique for Order Preference by Similarity to Ideal Solution);- Multicriteria optimization.
